Change PIN

The Change PIN page allows you to change your PIN.  The change is permanent and the newly specified PIN will be used in Multi-Factor Authentication going forward.

 

To change your PIN:

  1. Enter a new PIN.

  2. Enter the new PIN again to confirm.

  3. Click the Save button.

 

Feature

Description

New PIN

Type a new PIN.  Your administrator may have configured PIN rules such as a minimum length.

 

Confirm PIN

Retype the new PIN to confirm the change. Confirm PIN must match exactly.
